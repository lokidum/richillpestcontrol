The white ant work, done properly.

thermostat

Thermal imaging

Termites generate heat where they nest. The thermal camera shows it through the wall, no drilling, no guesswork.

water_drop

Moisture meters

Termites need moisture. We map the wet spots so we know where they are heading, not just where they have been.

radar

Termatrac radar

Microwave radar detects movement inside the timber. Confirms activity without opening up the wall.

videocam

Borescope camera

Tiny camera on a flexible cable for tight spots like roof voids, sub-floors, and skirting cavities.

Treatment plans we use

  • shield
    Chemical termite barriers. Trenched and treated around the perimeter using APVMA approved transfer products. 8 year warranty options.
  • construction
    Physical barriers. Installed during construction or renovation. Termimesh, granite particle, the lot.
  • my_location
    Baiting systems. In-ground stations around the property. Sentricon, Exterra, Nemesis depending on what suits the site.
  • healing
    Active colony treatment. Direct foam or dust into the live workings before we move to a barrier or bait plan.
  • event_repeat
    Annual monitoring. Yearly inspection on the book, so anything new gets caught early.
Perth pest calendar

When to book what.

Perth has its own rhythm. A rough guide to what shows up when, so you can book ahead instead of chase.

Spring

Sep to Nov

Everything wakes up. The big one for prevention work.

  • Termites swarming
  • Ants on the march
  • European wasps build nests
  • Annual termite inspection ideal

Summer

Dec to Feb

Hot, dry, and busy. The peak season.

  • Flies, mozzies, midges
  • Redback & whitetail spiders
  • Cockroaches at their worst
  • Snake season (refer licensed catcher)

Autumn

Mar to May

They move inside. Best time for indoor work.

  • Portuguese millipedes invade
  • Rats & mice come inside
  • Silverfish in bookshelves
  • Carpet beetles & clothes moths

Winter

Jun to Aug

Quieter outside, but rodents and termites don't sleep.

  • Roof rat & mice peak
  • Subterranean termites active
  • Pre-purchase inspections
  • Bird proofing before nesting

Not sure which season your problem belongs to? It's almost certainly active right now. Ring Mike on 0426 427 299.

FAQs

Questions we get a lot.

How much is a pest control visit? add

Most general treatments land around $250 for a standard Perth home. Termite inspections are quoted on the property, bigger homes and bigger jobs cost more. Whatever it is, we tell you the number upfront. No surprises at the end.

Is it safe for my kids and pets? add

Yes, with one caveat. We use APVMA approved products that are low toxicity for humans and pets, and where the job allows we use low tox or chemical free options on top of that. We'll always ask about little ones, pets, and any wildlife in the yard before we choose a treatment, and we'll walk you through what to do for an hour or two after we leave.

How long until the pests are gone? add

Cockroaches and ants thin out across the first week as they walk through the treatment back to the nest. Rats and mice usually clear inside 7 to 14 days. Bed bugs need two visits over a fortnight. Termite treatments depend on the plan, but you'll know exactly what to expect before we start.

Do you offer a guarantee? add

Yes. If the same pests come back inside the warranty window on your job, we come back and treat it again. No second invoice, no quibbling.

Do you do termite inspections? add

Inspections are our speciality. We use thermal imaging and moisture meters, and we write a report to the AS 3660.2 standard so you can use it for a property purchase if you need to.

Do you do low tox or chemical free pest control? add

Yes. Reduced chemistry is one of the things we built the business around. Baiting, exclusion, targeted treatments. We'll tell you honestly which jobs suit a low tox approach and which ones genuinely need a chemical treatment.
