Our speciality

Termite inspections and treatment

Full inspection with thermal imaging and moisture readings, written report you can take to a bank or an agent, and a treatment plan that fits your house, not a template. We do AS 3660.2 standard reports.

pest_control

General pest control

The seasonal all-rounder. Inside and outside treatment that knocks back ants, cockroaches, spiders, silverfish and the usual Perth offenders. Most homes book this once or twice a year.

eco

Low tox & chemical free options

Where the job allows, we use baiting, exclusion work, and reduced-chemical treatments. Great for homes with little kids, pets, native lizards in the yard, or anyone who just prefers less chemistry.

home_work

Pre purchase termite inspection

Buying a place? We inspect before settlement, write a report that meets the standard, and walk you through the risks in plain English. Worth doing on every Perth purchase, not just the suspect ones.

pets

Rats and mice

Bait stations placed where they should be, entry points blocked, and a return visit to make sure they're gone. We're careful about bait placement near native wildlife.

bug_report

Cockroaches

German cockroaches in the kitchen need a different plan to the big bush ones outside, so we treat to species. Gel baits inside, residual on the perimeter, and a chat about what's drawing them in.

linear_scale

Ants

Find the nest, treat the trail, seal the entry. If it's white ants it's not really an ant, see termite inspections above.

grid_3x3

Spiders

Web sweep, residual on eaves and skirting, and an extra look around kids play areas. Redbacks and whitetails get the close attention they deserve.

bed

Bed bugs

Mattress, frame, skirting, the lot. Bed bugs are the one job you can't half do, so we book the time it actually takes.

cruelty_free

Fleas

Inside, outside, and a chat about the pets. Usually two visits to break the breeding cycle.

hive

Wasps and bees

Wasps we remove. Bees we'll relocate where we can, because we like bees more than we like exterminating them. European wasps sorted promptly.

key

End of lease pest treatment

Real estate signed report, completed in one visit, scheduled around your move date. Flea certificates done where the lease asks for one.

Questions we get a lot

How much is a pest control visit? expand_more

Most general treatments land around $250 for a standard Perth home. Termite inspections are quoted on the property, bigger homes and bigger jobs cost more. Whatever it is, we tell you the number upfront. No surprises at the end.

Is it safe for my kids and pets? expand_more

Yes. We use APVMA approved products that are low toxicity for humans and pets, and where the job allows we use low tox or chemical free options on top of that. We'll always ask about little ones, pets, and any wildlife in the yard before we choose a treatment, and we'll walk you through what to do for an hour or two after we leave.

Do you offer a guarantee? expand_more

Yes. If the same pests come back inside the warranty window on your job, we come back and treat it again. No second invoice, no quibbling.

Do you do termite inspections? expand_more

Inspections are our speciality. We use thermal imaging and moisture meters, and we write a report to the AS 3660.2 standard so you can use it for a property purchase if you need to.
